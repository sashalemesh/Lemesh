Food

MAINS

Our meat is sourced from producers across australia with an importance on quality our head chef, lachy cashman, has a long and detailed history with butchery, growing up on a cattle farm and following in the footsteps of his family trade we are continually looking for the choicest, most premium meat available the preparation of our meat includes dry aging our beef in a temperature and humidity controlled room.

By dry aging the beef it concentrates the flavour and increases the tenderness, enabling us to provide you with the highest quality possible.
